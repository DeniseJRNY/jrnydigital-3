10 Best SEO Tools Every Beginner Should Use in 2024

Visibility is everything, especially for businesses. Did you know nearly 70% of online journeys start with a search engine? That's a huge pool of potential customers who could be searching for exactly what you offer, but only if your website shows up in the top search results.

Here's the kicker: the top result gets 10 times more clicks than the 10th. Definitely, SEO (search engine optimisation) is your golden ticket to drawing in those eager customers and boosting your sales. Ready to dive in? We've got the top 10 SEO tools for 2024 all lined up to help you grow your business like never before.

Why Invest in SEO Tools?

SEO involves a multi-faceted approach, and tools streamline this process by simplifying website optimisation and providing valuable data. They offer insights on:

Keyword popularity Competitor strategies Website performance

With these data-driven insights, you can craft a winning SEO strategy and watch your website climb the search engine rankings effectively.

Best Free SEO Tools in 2024

Google Search Console

Google Search Console is a free, must-have tool that helps you understand how Google views your site. It's perfect for spotting issues that could impact your ranking. It also provides insights into your site's performance.

Price: Free
Features: Site performance tracking, search traffic analysis, issue detection and alerts, and insights into Google's indexing of your site.
Great for: Business owners, startups, and SEO professionals looking to optimise their site's visibility in Google searches and monitor their site's health.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics goes deeper into analysing how users interact with your site. It's invaluable for understanding visitor behaviour and traffic sources, which can guide your content and SEO strategy.

Price: Free
Features: In-depth analysis of user interaction, visitor behaviour, and traffic sources.
Great for: Businesses seeking to understand audience needs and optimise their content and SEO strategies.

Wordstream Free Keyword Tool

Wordstream Free Keyword Tool is a straightforward option for generating keyword ideas and understanding search volumes and competition. It's particularly user-friendly for beginners, making it a great starting point for startups looking to dive into SEO and find opportunities to attract more traffic.

Price: Free
Features: Keyword idea generation, search volume, and competition analysis.
Great for: Beginners and startups entering the SEO space.

Answer the Public

Answer the Public is a free tool that generates content ideas from search queries, helping you create relevant and engaging content for your audience. It's particularly useful for startups looking to establish thought leadership and connect with their audience through valuable, search-optimised content.

Price: Free
Features: Content idea generation based on search queries.
Great for: Startups establishing thought leadership with engaging content.

PageSpeed Insights

PageSpeed Insights analyses your website's loading speed and offers recommendations to improve it. Fast loading times are crucial for user experience and SEO, making this tool essential for businesses aiming to keep their website performance optimal and reduce bounce rates.

Price: Free
Features: Website loading speed analysis and improvement suggestions.
Great for: Ensuring optimal website performance and user experience.

Best Paid SEO Tools in 2024

Ubersuggest

Ubersuggest combines free and paid features to provide keyword discovery and SEO insights. It's a valuable resource for understanding how to optimise your content and strategy to meet the needs of your target audience. For new businesses, Ubersuggest is an accessible way to start exploring SEO without a significant initial investment.

Price: Starts at $12 monthly
Features: Keyword discovery, SEO insights.
Great for: New businesses exploring SEO without a large upfront investment.

Ahrefs

This comprehensive toolkit offers a comprehensive suite for SEO, including keyword research, site audits, and competitor analysis. It's particularly useful for businesses looking to gain an edge over competitors by understanding their backlink profiles and the keywords they rank for. Ahrefs can help pinpoint what's needed to improve your search visibility and drive more targeted traffic to your site.

Price: Starts at $99 monthly
Features: Keyword research, site audits, competitor analysis, backlink tracking.
Great for: Businesses aiming to surpass competitors and enhance search visibility.

SEMRush

SEMRush provides extensive capabilities to evaluate your site's search performance and benchmark against competitors. With its keyword research and tracking features, startups can identify new opportunities and monitor trends over time.

Price: Starts at $129.95 monthly
Features: Site search performance evaluation, keyword research, trend monitoring.
Great for: Startups and businesses focusing on market positioning and SEO optimisation.

Moz Pro

Moz Pro offers a robust set of tools for site audits, backlink analysis, and keyword recommendations. It's designed to help businesses improve their search rankings and online visibility. Moz Pro is especially beneficial for startups looking for detailed insights into SEO metrics and actionable advice on enhancing their web presence.

Price: Starts at $99 monthly
Features: Site audits, backlink analysis, keyword recommendations.
Great for: Startups and companies needing detailed SEO insights and improvement strategies.

Surfer SEO

Surfer SEO uses AI to analyse and compare your content against competitors, offering recommendations for optimisation. This tool is key for businesses focused on content marketing, helping ensure your articles and web pages are fully optimised for search engines and readers alike.

Price: Starts at $89 monthly
Features: AI-driven content analysis and optimisation recommendations.
Great for: Content-focused businesses aiming for optimised web presence.

How to Pick the Best SEO Tool for You

With so many options available, choosing the right SEO tool can feel like a daunting task. Here are some key factors to consider:

1. Your Needs and Goals

Defining your SEO goals is crucial for choosing the right tools and strategies. Ask yourself:

  • What are your primary SEO objectives? Do you aim to increase website traffic, rank higher for specific keywords, develop effective lead generation SEO strategies, or boost your local SEO presence?
  • What is your current level of SEO knowledge? Choose tools that cater to your skill set. Opt for user-friendly interfaces and tutorials if needed.

By understanding your specific goals and skill level, you can select the best SEO tools to empower your online presence and achieve your desired outcomes.

2. Budget

SEO tools range from free to premium options with varying features and functionalities. Remember, SEO is an investment that can yield significant returns in terms of improved website traffic, leads, and sales. Consider your budget and prioritise features relevant to your needs, ensuring they directly contribute to your SEO goals. Many tools offer free trials, allowing you to test them before committing.

Pro Tip: Partner with an SEO agency for maximum ROI

SEO costs in Australia can vary depending on the agency's experience, the scope of services offered, and the size of your website. However, investing in a qualified SEO agency like JRNY Digital can be a strategic decision, potentially yielding a significant return on your investment in the long run.

3. Website Platform

Some SEO tools integrate better with specific website platforms like WordPress or Shopify. Ensure the tool you choose is compatible with your website platform for seamless integration.

4. Features

Review the features offered by each tool and prioritise those essential for your goals. Consider features like keyword research, competitor analysis, backlink monitoring, on-page optimisation assistance, and content creation tools.

5. User Reviews and Ratings

Read user reviews and ratings to gain insights into the effectiveness and user experience of different tools. This can help you narrow down your choices and select a tool that meets your expectations.
